How to use:

1. Enter your favorite symbol in the first cell of the title option. The person with that symbol can rename the notification.

2. Enter a name of your choice in the second cell of the title option. The name you enter there will be applied in the notification field.

3. If you want to hide the message in the notification field, turn on "Change message" in the message options.

4. If you turn on "Change message", you can enter the changed text in the second cell of the message options.

5. Select the application to apply from "Select App ...".

6. Respring \(*・ω・*)/
